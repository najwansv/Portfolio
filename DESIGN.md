Design System: Shader Studio Aesthetic

This design system is inspired by the creative development studio, Shader. It captures a unique blend of retro-tech nostalgia and futuristic minimalism, perfect for a high-impact personal portfolio.

Brand Personality





Sophisticated & Experimental: A balance of high-end design and creative exploration.



Nostalgic-Futuristic: Utilizing retro computing motifs (like CRT monitors) paired with modern, sleek web layouts.



High-Contrast Dark Mode: Deep blacks and grays as the foundation, allowing warm typography and glowing elements to stand out.

Color Palette





Deep Space Black (#0a0a0a): The primary background color.



Surface Gray (#1a1a1a): For secondary sections, cards, and UI containers.



Warm White / Cream (#f5f5f0): The primary typography color, providing a soft but high-contrast look.



Accent Glow (#ffcc00): A subtle warm amber or gold for hover states, links, and specialized highlights, mimicking the glow of a vintage display.

Typography





Primary Heading (Serif): Newsreader or Playfair Display. Used for hero statements and large titles to convey elegance and craftsmanship.



Secondary Heading & UI (Sans-Serif): Inter or Geist. Clean, highly legible, and modern for navigation, subheaders, and body text.



Monospace Accent: JetBrains Mono or Roboto Mono. Used sparingly for technical details, metadata, or to reinforce the "development studio" feel.

Visual Elements & UI Style





Minimal Roundness: Very low border radius (2px to 4px) or completely sharp corners to maintain a structured, professional feel.



Grain & Texture: Subtle film grain or noise overlays to add depth and a tactile quality to the dark backgrounds.



Soft Glows: Use of box-shadow or drop-shadow with low opacity and high blur to create "plugged-in" lighting effects around key images or text.



Micro-Interactions: Smooth, deliberate transitions. Hover effects that include slight scaling or color shifts towards the accent glow.

Imagery





3D Renderings: High-quality, atmospheric 3D scenes with focused lighting.



Retro Tech: Icons or visuals featuring 80s/90s computing hardware to emphasize the "plugged into the future" theme.
